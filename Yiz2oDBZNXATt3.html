Jan 12, 2023

Osogbo Central Hospital Official Commissioning by Gov. Adeleke

All roads will today, Thursday January 12, 2023 lead to the premises of the Osogbo Central Hospital, Oke-Baale, Osogbo, capital of Osun State for the official commissioning of the medical facility.

The ultramodern state is the earth edifice was completed in 2021 while management of the hospital, in a bid to continue to offer medical services to the people of the state and outside of the state, moved into the facility on September 1, 2021.

Sequel to the founder's dream of having a 21st century taste hospital in 2017 that would parade modern equipment for treatment of all kinds of ailment, the name of the hospital was changed from Oke-Baale Central Hospital to Osogbo Central Hospital.

The group specialist hospital, accredited by National Health Insurance Authority (NHIA) and Osun Health Insurance Agency (OSHIA) will be commissioned today as the who is who in the state and the medical profession are billed to grace the occasion.

The programme starts at 10am according to the management.
