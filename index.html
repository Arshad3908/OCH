About us

who we are

Osogbo Central
Hospital

Osogbo Central Hospital is a group specialist hospital that works twenty-four hours (24hrs) making patients interest of a top most priority, we are a team of doctors that has a vision of creating a first class healthcare services to patients and a mission of making patients care the best among its pairs.
Motto: God our Pillar and Strength.

It is a private owned institution run by a group of specialists in view of giving standard health care delivery to her patients. The hospital is located at Adjacent Nawarudeen Grammer School, along Ibokun road Oke-baale, Osogbo, Osun state, Nigeria.

departments

our departments

Dialysis

We have well equiped dialysis unit with well trained nurses and Nephrologist.

EMERGENCY AND AMBULANCE SERVICES

Ambulance is accessible to convey emergency patients to the hospital within the state for free. We also have ambulance with a transport ventilator. Simply ICU on wheel

Dental

We offer dental and maxillofacial surgery, Trauma care to patients of all ages. Orthodontics services also available

Ophtalmology

Our experienced Optometrists and Optical staff are always happy to see you at Osogbo Central Hospital

Intensive Care Unit

We provides intensive care/life support treatments to patients with severe and life threatening illness and injuries that require close monitoring and support.Transport ventilator also available.

Medical Gym

Well equipped with standard facilities that operates under the guidance of cardiologists, sports and medicine experts and physiotherapists.

OBSTETRICS AND GYNAECOLOGY

As one of the major areas of preventing maternal mortality, our team of experts focuses on the complete care of women and their reproductive system, including pre, ante and post natal care. Infertility care also available

Laboratory

We are equipped with advanced clinical laboratory equipment capable of performing a wide range of haematology, immunoassay, biochemistry, microbiology and pathology tests.

Radiology

The Radiology Department offers a comprehensive range of imaging tests in support of the clinical practices in the hospital. The hospital has Ultrasound machines of different categories, Digital X-Ray, Orthopantomogram (OPG) amidst others.

PSYCHIATRY

We evaluate, diagnose and treat patients with mental, emotional and behavioral disorder and also work with patients to develop a management plan for recovery. Electroconvulsive Theraphy (ECT) machine also available. Resident Consultant Psychiatrist also available. We offer Psychotherapy, Rehabilitation, Counceling, amidst other care

SCBU

We have a state of the art neonatology unit and a team of experts that deals with neonatal illnesses and the child’s physical, mental and behavioral developments. Incubator service is avaibale

Cardiology

Electrocardiography (ECG) and Echocardiography (ECHO) services aress available
